Open .csv spreadsheet.

In Matlab, create an empty variable called paperyear. Paste two columns of data into it.

It's reverse chronological (again) but that is okay.

x=paperyear(:,1); y=paperyear(:,2);

plot(x,y,'o')

c1=polyfit(x,y,1)

y1=polyval(c1,x);

c2=polyfit(x,y,2);

y2=polyval(c2,x);

c3=polyfit(x,y,3);

y3=polyval(c3,x);

plot(x,y,'o',x,y1,x,y2,x,y3)

legend('data','n=1','n=2','n=3')

grid

recall that R^2 = 1 - SSE/SST

ymean=mean(y);

SST=norm(y-ymean)^2
